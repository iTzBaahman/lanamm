My dearest Lana,

There are a thousand things I want to say to you, and yet when I am near you, words feel too small — too pale to carry the weight of what I feel. So I write this, hoping the ink carries what my voice sometimes cannot.

You are the kind of person who makes the world softer. The way you laugh, the way you think, the way you care — these are not small things. They are the things I think about when I want to remember what is beautiful about life.

I want you to know that you are seen. Completely. Every part of you — the parts you show the world and the parts you keep close — I see them, and I love all of it. There is no version of you that I do not love.

Thank you for being exactly who you are. Thank you for letting me be part of your life. Every day with you is a gift I do not take lightly.

You deserve every happiness this world has to offer. And for as long as I am here, I will do my best to bring it to you.

Forever yours 💕

❤️

Lana — you are loved, deeply and endlessly.
